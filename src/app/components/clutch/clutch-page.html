H-O-U CLUTCH

Welcome to the Houston Clutch

As a dynamic and diverse group of athletes, we are dedicated to the thrilling and strategic sport of Ultimate Frisbee. Our team is not just about playing a game; it's about building a community, fostering sportsmanship, and pushing each other to new heights.

Goals

  • Achieve Competitive Success: Strive for excellence in regional and national tournaments, showcasing our skills and teamwork.
  • Promote the Sport: Raise awareness and enthusiasm for Ultimate Frisbee within the Houston community, encouraging participation at all levels.
  • Personal Development: Support each player's growth, emphasizing physical fitness, strategic thinking, and leadership skills.

Expectations

  • Commitment: Regular attendance at practices and games, dedication to improvement, and a positive attitude.
  • Teamwork: Foster a collaborative spirit, respect for teammates and opponents, and effective communication on and off the field.
  • Integrity: Uphold the principles of fair play, honesty, and respect that are the cornerstone of Ultimate Frisbee.

Inspiration

  • Community: The vibrant Houston community and our loyal supporters who cheer us on at every match.
  • Legends of the Sport: Iconic players and teams who have elevated Ultimate Frisbee to new heights, inspiring us to reach for greatness.
  • Our Journey: Each practice, game, and tournament is a step toward our collective and individual goals, driving us to push boundaries and achieve our best.

Join us in our exciting journey as we chase victories, celebrate our love for the game, and build lasting friendships. Together, we are more than just a team; we are the Houston Ultimate Frisbee family.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Clutch is going into its sixth club season year and we are looking for players interested in joining a competitive club team based out of Houston, TX. Our goal is to assemble a team of players that are willing to fully buy-in and put in the work to be mentally and physically competitive. Having a good time along the way goes without saying!
